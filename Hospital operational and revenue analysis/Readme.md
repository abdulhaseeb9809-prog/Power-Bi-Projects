#📊 Hospital Operations & Financial Analytics Dashboard

##🧾 Project Overview

This project analyzes hospital operational and financial performance using Power BI. The objective is to identify inefficiencies in doctor workload distribution, department utilization, patient wait time, and appointment no-show rates, and to provide actionable insights for improved decision-making.

##🎯 Problem Statement

Hospitals generate large volumes of data across appointments, treatments, and departments, but often lack clear visibility into operational inefficiencies such as:

Uneven doctor workload distribution
Underutilized department capacity
Increasing patient wait times
Rising no-show rates

This project transforms raw hospital data into meaningful insights to improve operational efficiency and patient experience.

##📁 Dataset

The analysis is based on synthetic hospital data, including:

Appointment records (date, doctor, department, status)
Treatment and revenue data
Department capacity information
Doctor seniority details
Patient wait time and satisfaction metrics

##⚙️ Tools & Technologies

Power BI – Dashboard development
Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Measures & calculations

##📊 Dashboard Structure

###1️⃣ Financial Overview
Total Hospital Revenue
Appointment vs Treatment Revenue
Revenue trends over time
Department-wise contribution

###2️⃣ Operational Efficiency
Department Utilization %
Patient Wait Time
No-Show Rate
Doctor Load Distribution

###3️⃣ Workforce & Strategic Insights
Revenue by Doctor Seniority
Profit contribution by doctors
Doctor Load vs Average (workload imbalance)

##🔍 Key Insights

Revenue is highly concentrated among a small group of senior doctors
Significant workload imbalance exists among doctors
Department utilization is below optimal capacity
Patient wait time shows an increasing trend in certain periods
No-show rates have risen, impacting operational efficiency

##💡 Business Recommendations

Redistribute appointments to balance doctor workload
Improve scheduling to reduce patient waiting time
Implement reminder systems to reduce no-shows
Optimize department capacity utilization
Use predictive models for demand and workload forecasting

##🚀 Future Scope
Integrate real-time hospital data sources
Develop predictive models for patient demand forecasting
Build no-show prediction systems
Implement workload forecasting for better doctor allocation
Create early warning alerts for operational issues
